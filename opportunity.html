As an iGalen Team Member, you proudly represent iGalen’s innovative products. We believe the iGalen opportunity is second to none for those that want to earn a serious part-time income or make a full-time career out of it.

The rewarding iGalen compensation plan encompasses six key components for long-term prosperity:

1. Retail Profit – Earn up to 35% by purchasing products at wholesale price and selling them at retail price, you keep the profit!

2. Fast Start Bonus – Pays you immediate income on the initial order of the people you bring into your iGalen business!

3. Team Commission – Calculated on your placement tree, which has two legs: a left and a right. You will be compensated based on successfully building two legs of your own. I may even place people in your group, and these people will count towards your team commissions and rank qualification, a true team building approach!

4. Leadership Matching Bonus – Earn a matching bonus based on a percentage of team commissions from people that you personally sponsor, up to 5 levels deep!

5. Leadership Bonus Pool – For members ranking Ruby or above. Number of levels and percentages override are based on the ranking of the member!

6. Lifestyle Bonus – With iGalen’s Lifestyle Bonus, starting with the rank of Ruby, you will qualify to receive a monthly lifestyle bonus which can be used towards a new car payment or other luxury expenses!
